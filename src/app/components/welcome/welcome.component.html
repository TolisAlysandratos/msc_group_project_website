Welcome to moodify.

The crowdsourced application that generates music playlists based on moods.

As our application uses Spotify, we need your permission to connect Moodify with your Spotify account.

Connecting your Spotify account to our app:

1. Allows you to instantly add to your account the playlists you enjoy with a single click.
2. Does not affect your Spotify account in any other way.
3. Does not allow us access to any account information other than your publically available Spotify User ID.
